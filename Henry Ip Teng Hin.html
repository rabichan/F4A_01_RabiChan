Hello, I am Henry.

I am a registered secondary school teacher based in Macao, teaching Economics, Business, Accounting and ICT. I have over 6 years of teaching experience across junior and senior secondary levels.

Teaching and learning happen everywhere, not just in the classroom. My YouTube channel "Econ Outsider" is one of the most popular channels producing economics tutorials in Macau and Hong Kong.

Applying theories into practice is the essence of learning economics. "Economics Study Log" is one of the first economics publications in secondary education in Macau.

Students of mine not only achieve excellent results in academics, but also in various competitions such as "National Economics Challenge" and "Consumer Study Award".
